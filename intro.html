About Me

Greetings! I am Dr. Pratibha Pandey, a passionate educator and dedicated researcher at N.I.E.T., with a background in Cancer Biology and Biotechnology, I bring 9 years of experience in Teaching and Research. My journey in academia has been shaped by a commitment to fostering a dynamic learning environment and promoting intellectual curiosity among students.
I believe that education is a collaborative process, where both teachers and students engage in a dialogue to explore new horizons of understanding. As a researcher, my interests lie in Cancer Biology. I am driven by my parents. My work has been published in reputable journals and presented at various conferences. Beyond academia, I enjoy singing and my interest lies in spirituality, which I find to be a source of inspiration and balance in my life.
I am thrilled to be a part of the vibrant academic community at N.I.E.T., where I continue to learn, grow, and contribute to the advancement of knowledge.
